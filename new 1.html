Super Dad

He is no one other than Mr Wasiu Lawal and as i've said before, he's a statistician and works at Abuja for the federal Government.

He really is an amazing person, if you get to meet him in person. He was grew up in Lagos island, In the ghetto part, better known or looks like a slum due to to crammed up houses and touts. Being a part of that kind of environment made him know what its like to be a street kid, hooligan and a street vagabond, but thanks To God,due to his parents and some personal strict decisions, He grew up to be a God-fearing, responsible, loving, caring and sometimes clueless Father.

Growing up with him was a fun and cheery time but he was not always around. He's a great teacher and i can prodly say most of my academical feats and glories especially in anything mathematical was learnt from him. Right from the days of quantitative Reasoning to the days of Further maths, he's been my personal tutor. Every other weekend or spare evenings was spent grilling his children to be academically and Morally upright

You gotta love the way children get to love him and cling to him, due to his good hearted nature and obvious love for kids. Its also rare to see his other scary side easily. Like every person, He has his own short comings and bad moods, but luckily for me, i've only managed to get there and escape unscated a few times. But stii being the kid i was and still am, i got the right form of discipline i deserved when i deserved them. I dont envy or wish to be in the position of anyone that gets inhis bad books.

A True family man and head of a family, he's the best dad i've ever known and i hope and pray to be like him someday.
